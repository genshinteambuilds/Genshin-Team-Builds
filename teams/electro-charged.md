# Electro-Charged (Electro)

This team composition focuses on the **Electro-Charged Elemental Reaction** that requires **Hydro** and **Electro** to be applied to an enemy. This reaction deals **Electro DMG** over time to target affected by the reaction as well as to any nearby targets that are **Wet**.

This page contains **Electro-Charged** teams with a **Electro** Main DPS character. For **Electro-Charged** teams with **Hydro** Main DPS characters, see [Electro-Charged (Hydro)](electro-charged-hydro.md).

## Sample Team Compositions

#### ![](../.gitbook/assets/UI\_AvatarIcon\_Yae.png)![](../.gitbook/assets/UI\_AvatarIcon\_Kokomi.png)![](../.gitbook/assets/UI\_AvatarIcon\_Kazuha.png)![](../.gitbook/assets/UI\_AvatarIcon\_Shougun.png)

* Yae provides the majority of the damage with her **Elemental Skill** and **Burst.**
* Kokomi's **Elemental Skill** and **Burst** provides Yae with sufficient **Hydro** application to continuously sustain **Electro-Charged**.
* Kazuha provides buffing to **Electro DMG** after causing **Swirl** and an **Elemental RES** debuff with the **Viridescent Venerer** 4-set.
* Raiden uses her **Elemental Burst** to provide energy for the entire party and damage while Yae is Off-Field.

#### ![](../.gitbook/assets/UI\_AvatarIcon\_Keqing.png)![](../.gitbook/assets/UI\_AvatarIcon\_Beidou.png)![](../.gitbook/assets/UI\_AvatarIcon\_Venti.png)![](../.gitbook/assets/UI\_AvatarIcon\_Kokomi.png)

* Keqing provides the majority of On-Field **DMG** with her **Electro** infused **Charged Attacks**.
* Kokomi acts as both the Core Support and the healer of the party, providing **Hydro** application and allowing for an additional Flex Support to be added.
* Beidou provides additional Off-Field **Electro DMG** and enables **Electro Resonance**.
* Venti provides an **Elemental RES** debuff with the **Viridescent Venerer** 4-set as well as Grouping Crowd Control and additional energy when he infuses his **Elemental Burst** with **Electro**.

#### ![](../.gitbook/assets/UI\_AvatarIcon\_Shougun.png)![](../.gitbook/assets/UI\_AvatarIcon\_Xingqiu.png)![](../.gitbook/assets/UI\_AvatarIcon\_Sara.png)![](../.gitbook/assets/UI\_AvatarIcon\_Jean.png)

* Raiden's role in this team is to obtain buffs from other party members before attacking with her **Elemental Burst**.
* Xingqiu provides constant **Hydro** application to keep **Electro-Charged** on enemies as well as additional Off-Field DPS.
* Sara enables Raiden through her **ATK** buff and **C6 Electro CRIT DMG** buff and provides **Electro Resonance** for additional energy.
* Jean provides an **Elemental RES** debuff with the **Viridescent Venerer** 4-set and healing.
