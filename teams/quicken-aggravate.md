# Quicken (Aggravate)

{% hint style="warning" %}
**This Page is under construction.**
{% endhint %}

**Quicken (Aggravate)** teams focus on frequently applying **Electro** to **Quickened** targets **** to trigger the **Aggravate** reaction. Despite having a lower damage multiplier than **Spread**, **Electro**-based **Aggravate** teams can cause the reaction more consistently, which can lead to better damage.

{% hint style="danger" %}
**Characters are ranked roughly according to how well they perform in this team composition.**
{% endhint %}
