# Vaporize (Pyro)

This comp focuses on using the **Vaporize Elemental Reaction** with a **Pyro** On-Field **DPS** and **Hydro Support** with the goal of **Vaporizing** as many attacks as possible in order to obtain a 1.5x **DMG** multiplier on those attacks.
