# Reverse Vaporize

This comp focuses on using the Vaporize reaction with a Main Pyro DPS and Hydro Support with the goal of vaporizing as many attacks as possible in order to obtain a 1.5x Damage Multiplier on those attacks.

{% hint style="danger" %}
Xingqiu is almost irreplaceable for this team composition. [See the notes on Xingqiu and Mona below.](reverse-vaporize.md#core-support)
{% endhint %}

{% hint style="info" %}
Characters are ranked roughly according to how well they perform in this team composition.

Most of the characters within a given role are interchangeable except if mentioned above.\
\
Refer to the character notes below for how characters work in this particular team composition or click on their portraits for a description of how the character works in general.
{% endhint %}

## Core Support

|                                                                                      |                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       |
| :----------------------------------------------------------------------------------: | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| <p><img src="../.gitbook/assets/UI_AvatarIcon_Xingqiu.png" alt=""></p><p>Xingqiu</p> | <ul><li>Plays a virtually irreplaceable core role in a Reverse Vaporize Team.</li><li>Applies Hydro incredibly frequently with his Elemental Burst allowing Pyro DPS characters to vaporize their attacks for 1.5x their normal damage.</li><li>Xingqiu's Elemental Skill and Elemental Burst also deal a large amount of damage on their own when properly geared.</li></ul>                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
|    <p><img src="../.gitbook/assets/UI_AvatarIcon_Mona.png" alt=""></p><p>Mona</p>    | <ul><li>Mona cannot apply Hydro as often as Xingqiu which will often result in Pyro DPS characters being unable to vaporize consistently. Any vaporizes being triggered correctly are typically by accident.</li><li>Mona's primary use is to boost damage through the application of Omen caused by her Elemental Burst which can be used to boost Klee's Charged Attack Damage or the Elemental Bursts of the other Pryo DPS characters.</li><li>Mona's "bubble" Immobilization Crowd Control effect from Omen also causes large amounts of damage when broken and can also be vaporized.</li></ul> |
