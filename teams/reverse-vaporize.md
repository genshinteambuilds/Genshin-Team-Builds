# Reverse Vaporize

This comp focuses on using the Vaporize reaction with a Main Pyro DPS and Hydro Support with the goal of vaporizing as many attacks as possible in order to obtain a 1.5x Damage Multiplier on those attacks.

{% hint style="danger" %}
Xingqiu is almost irreplaceable for this team composition. [See the notes on Xingqiu and Mona below.](reverse-vaporize.md#core-support)
{% endhint %}

{% hint style="info" %}
Characters are ranked roughly according to how well they perform in this team composition.

Most of the characters within a given role are interchangeable except if mentioned above.  
  
Refer to the character notes below for how characters work in this particular team composition or click on their portraits for a description of how the character works in general.
{% endhint %}

## Core Support

<table>
  <thead>
    <tr>
      <th style="text-align:center"></th>
      <th style="text-align:left"></th>
    </tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr>
      <td style="text-align:center">
        <p>
          <img src="../.gitbook/assets/ui_avataricon_xingqiu.png" alt/>
        </p>
        <p>Xingqiu</p>
      </td>
      <td style="text-align:left">
        <ul>
          <li>Plays a virtually irreplaceable core role in a Reverse Vaporize Team.</li>
          <li>Applies Hydro incredibly frequently with his Elemental Burst allowing
            Pyro DPS characters to vaporize their attacks for 1.5x their normal damage.</li>
          <li>Xingqiu&apos;s Elemental Skill and Elemental Burst also deal a large amount
            of damage on their own when properly geared.</li>
        </ul>
      </td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td style="text-align:center">
        <p>
          <img src="../.gitbook/assets/ui_avataricon_mona.png" alt/>
        </p>
        <p>Mona</p>
      </td>
      <td style="text-align:left">
        <ul>
          <li>Mona cannot apply Hydro as often as Xingqiu which will often result in
            Pyro DPS characters being unable to vaporize consistently. Any vaporizes
            being triggered correctly are typically by accident.</li>
          <li>Mona&apos;s primary use is to boost damage through the application of
            Omen caused by her Elemental Burst which can be used to boost Klee&apos;s
            Charged Attack Damage or the Elemental Bursts of the other Pryo DPS characters.</li>
          <li>Mona&apos;s &quot;bubble&quot; Immobilization Crowd Control effect from
            Omen also causes large amounts of damage when broken and can also be vaporized.</li>
        </ul>
      </td>
    </tr>
  </tbody>
</table>
