# Pure Electro

This team composition focuses on **Electro** characters that center around dealing purely **Electro** **DMG**. The **Electro DPS** will be mainly paired with **Electro**, **Anemo,** and **Geo** support characters. These supports will be to increase the effectiveness of dealing **Electro DMG** and may also provide Off-Field DPS and healing.&#x20;

Swirl is the only notable **Elemental Reaction** as the **Viridescent Venerer** 4-set can reduce enemies' **Electro Resistance**.

This is viable when **Hydro** characters that are essential for **Electro-Charged Elemental Reactions** are scarce, see [Electro-Charged (Electro)](electro-charged.md) and [Electro-Charged (Hydro)](electro-charged-hydro.md).
