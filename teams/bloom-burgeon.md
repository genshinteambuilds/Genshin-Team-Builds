# Bloom (Burgeon)

**Burgeon** is a reaction caused by **Pyro** application to a **Dendro** core. **Burgeon** based team compositions include **Dendro, Hydro,** and **Pyro** characters, often with an **Anemo** or flex fourth slot. ****&#x20;

**Burgeon** damage is based on the **Elemental Mastery** of the character that applies **Pyro**.&#x20;
